ABOUT

I’m Johan Saeij, a landscape photographer based in Santa Barbara, California. I started taking pictures a little under a year ago and have developed a strong passion for both photography and the outdoors.

I currently shoot on a Sony A7III with a G Master 16–35mm f/2.8 lens. I also use a tripod and various filters to enhance my landscape shots. Over the past year, I’ve had the opportunity to visit some of the most beautiful places on Earth.

As I continue my photography journey, I hope to explore many more places and keep experiencing some of the world’s most breathtaking landscapes. Feel free to reach out if you have any questions.
